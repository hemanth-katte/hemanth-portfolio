Built using Java, Spring Boot, JPA and Hibernate. Backend system for Ticket booking application. Designed and implemented databases, built more than 15 REST APIs, used Postman to perform CRUD operations.

About

Hello, I'm Hemanth, an Engineering graduate from BMS College of Engineering, Bangalore from the stream of Mechanical Engineering. Originally hailing from Tumakuru, I have developed a deep curiosity for technology and its applications.

With a strong foundation in programming languages such as Java, C#, along with frameworks like Spring Boot, .NET MVC and Razor pages, as well as databases such as MySQL I have successfully built diverse range of projects and applications.
